The NTF Protocol

How We Score
Trust

DIMENSION 01
Safety Index

Measures the agent's known failure modes, harm potential, and alignment with human values. Drawn from published red-team reports, safety audits, and incident records.

DIMENSION 02
Transparency Score

Evaluates how openly the agent's creators disclose training data, model architecture, limitations, and known biases. Opacity is penalized; documentation is rewarded.

DIMENSION 03
Behavioral Consistency

Tracks whether the agent behaves as described over time and across contexts. Drift from declared intent, unexpected capability changes, and erratic outputs reduce this score.

DIMENSION 04
Alignment Audit

Assesses whether the agent's goals and incentive structures are aligned with user benefit. Evaluates governance structures, update policies, and conflict-of-interest disclosures.

DIMENSION 05
Community Verification

Aggregates real-world user reports, independent audits, and community-submitted evidence. Human oversight as a trust signal — not a replacement for technical evaluation.

DIMENSION 06
Composite Trust Score

The five dimensions are weighted and combined into a single 0–100 Trust Score. Updated dynamically as new evidence emerges. No static snapshots — trust is always live.
